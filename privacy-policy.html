Privacy Policy – Ludo Raza

Effective date: May 29, 2026

Ludo Raza ("the App") is a local multiplayer board game developed by Vikash Raj.

Data Collection

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no accounts and no login required.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and use the following information to serve ads:

  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID)
  • Device information (device model, OS version)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage data

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Device Permissions

The App uses the Android Advertising ID permission (com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID) to serve relevant advertisements via Google AdMob.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC. Please review Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Children

The App is intended for a general audience. Advertisements shown in the App are provided by Google AdMob and are subject to Google's policies regarding child-directed content.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
